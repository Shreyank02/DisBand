Welcome to the official website of DisBand, where the heart of rock beats louder than ever. With over a decade of trailblazing through the music scene, DisBand has become a global phenomenon, known for electrifying performances, chart-topping hits, and a unique sound that transcends genres. Our journey from garage band to international superstars has been fueled by passion, creativity, and the unwavering support of our incredible fans.

Our Music

Dive into the world of DisBand and explore our diverse discography. From our breakthrough debut album to our latest release, each record tells a story, blending powerful lyrics with unforgettable melodies. Our music spans across rock, alternative, and pop, reflecting our evolution as artists and our dedication to pushing the boundaries of sound.
